KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Arrhenius theory: acids give H+, bases give OH-, only in water
  • Bronsted-Lowry theory: acids donate protons, bases accept protons — works in any solvent
  • Every Bronsted-Lowry reaction creates a conjugate acid-base pair
  • Ammonia is a Bronsted-Lowry base despite having no OH- in its formula
