KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Lewis acid: any electron-pair acceptor (no proton required)
  • Lewis base: any electron-pair donor
  • Lewis theory is the broadest of the three: Arrhenius ⊂ Bronsted-Lowry ⊂ Lewis
  • BF3 + NH3 is a classic Lewis acid-base reaction with no protons involved
