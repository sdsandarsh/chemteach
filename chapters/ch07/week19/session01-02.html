KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • pH = -log[H+]; a logarithmic scale invented by Sorensen for practical convenience
  • pH < 7 acidic, pH = 7 neutral, pH > 7 basic (at 25°C)
  • pH + pOH = 14 at 25°C (since Kw = 10^-14)
  • Each whole pH unit represents a 10-fold change in [H+] concentration
