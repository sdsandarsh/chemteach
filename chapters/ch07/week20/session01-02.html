6.11.8 Common Ion Effect

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Common ion effect: adding an ion already present in equilibrium suppresses further ionization
  • Based directly on Le Chatelier's Principle applied to ionic equilibria
  • Always reduces degree of ionization of the weak acid/base
  • Forms the basis for how buffer solutions resist pH change
