KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Solubility Product (Ksp): equilibrium constant for dissolution of a sparingly soluble salt
  • Smaller Ksp = less soluble salt; larger Ksp = more soluble salt
  • Ksp = [cation]^x[anion]^y, exponents matching the dissolution equation
  • Comparing Ionic Product to Ksp predicts whether precipitation will occur
