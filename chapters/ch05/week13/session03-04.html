KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • First Law of Thermodynamics: ΔU = q + w — energy is conserved, only converted between forms
  • Sign convention: +q heat absorbed, +w work done on system
  • Free expansion: work done = 0, since external pressure = 0
  • This law forbids any 'perpetual motion machine' that creates energy from nothing
