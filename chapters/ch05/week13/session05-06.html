5.2.2 Enthalpy, Cp-Cv, Bomb Calorimeter

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Enthalpy: H = U + PV, designed so qp = ΔH at constant pressure
  • Bomb calorimeter: rigid, sealed, constant volume — measures ΔU directly
  • Cp − Cv = R for ideal gases; Cp is always greater than Cv
  • ΔH = ΔU + ΔnRT relates the two quantities for gas-phase reactions
