Topics covered today:
5.4 Enthalpy of Reaction, Standard States

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Standard states: 1 bar pressure, specified temperature (usually 298K), most stable form
  • Standard enthalpy of formation of an element in its stable form = 0 by definition
  • ΔrH° = Σ(ΔfH° products) − Σ(ΔfH° reactants)
  • Exothermic: ΔH negative (releases heat); Endothermic: ΔH positive (absorbs heat)
