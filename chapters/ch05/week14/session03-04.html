5.4(e) Hess's Law and Numericals

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Hess's Law: total ΔH for a reaction is the same regardless of the path/number of steps taken
  • This follows directly from enthalpy being a state function
  • Reversing a reaction flips the sign of ΔH; scaling a reaction scales ΔH proportionally
  • Used to calculate ΔH for reactions that are hard to measure directly
