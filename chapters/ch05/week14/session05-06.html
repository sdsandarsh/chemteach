5.5 Enthalpies: Combustion, Atomisation, Bond

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Enthalpy of combustion: always exothermic, complete burning in excess O2
  • Enthalpy of atomisation: always endothermic, breaking into free gaseous atoms
  • Bond enthalpy: energy per mole to break a specific bond in gaseous state
  • Bond breaking is always endothermic; bond formation is always exothermic
