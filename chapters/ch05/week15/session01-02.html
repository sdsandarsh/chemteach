KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Lattice enthalpy: always positive, energy to break ionic solid into gaseous ions
  • Enthalpy of hydration: always negative, energy released surrounding ions with water
  • Enthalpy of solution = lattice enthalpy + hydration enthalpy
  • Enthalpy of dilution: additional heat change on adding more solvent
