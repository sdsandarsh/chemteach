5.7 Gibbs Energy, Equilibrium Constant

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Gibbs Free Energy: ΔG = ΔH − TΔS, combines enthalpy and entropy into one spontaneity criterion
  • ΔG < 0: spontaneous; ΔG > 0: non-spontaneous; ΔG = 0: equilibrium
  • ΔG° = −RT ln K links Gibbs energy directly to the equilibrium constant
  • Temperature can flip a reaction's spontaneity if ΔH and ΔS have opposing signs
