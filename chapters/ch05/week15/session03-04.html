KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Entropy (S): measure of disorder/randomness in a system
  • Second Law: total entropy of the universe increases for any spontaneous process
  • Spontaneity depends on BOTH enthalpy and entropy, not energy alone
  • Entropy generally increases: solid < liquid < gas, and with increasing moles of gas
