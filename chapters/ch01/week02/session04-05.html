KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Dalton's 5 postulates: atoms are indivisible, identical within an element, combine in simple ratios, are neither created nor destroyed
  • Atomic mass: relative mass of an atom on the Carbon-12 scale (12C = 12 exactly)
  • Molecular mass: sum of atomic masses of all atoms in a molecule
  • Dalton's theory unified all 3 laws of chemical combination into one model
