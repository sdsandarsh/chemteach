1.8 Mole Concept — Introduction

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • A mole is a fixed count: 6.022 × 10^23 particles (Avogadro's number)
  • Molar mass = mass of 1 mole of a substance, in grams, numerically equal to atomic/molecular mass
  • Moles bridge the invisible atomic scale to the measurable laboratory scale
  • Number of moles = Given mass / Molar mass
