1.5 Laws of Chemical Combination

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Law of Conservation of Mass: mass is neither created nor destroyed in a reaction
  • Law of Definite Proportions: a given compound always has the same elements in the same mass ratio
  • Law of Multiple Proportions: two elements can form multiple compounds in simple whole-number mass ratios
  • Gay Lussac's Law: gases combine in simple ratios of volume at the same temperature and pressure
