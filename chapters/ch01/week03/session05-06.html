KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Stoichiometry: calculating reactant/product quantities using balanced equations
  • Limiting reagent: the reactant that is fully consumed first, capping product formed
  • Excess reagent: the reactant left over after the reaction stops
  • Always calculate yield using the limiting reagent's moles only
