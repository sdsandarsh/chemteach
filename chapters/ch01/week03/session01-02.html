1.8 Mole Concept — Molar Volume, Calculations

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • At STP, 1 mole of any gas occupies 22.4 litres (molar volume)
  • 3 routes to moles: mass/molar mass, volume/22.4 L, particles/Avogadro's number
  • Gas volume depends on particle count, not particle identity or mass
  • These three formulas are the toolkit for all mole-based numericals
