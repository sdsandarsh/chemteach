KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Percentage composition: mass percent of each element in a compound
  • Empirical formula: simplest whole-number atom ratio in a compound
  • Method: % → mass → moles → divide by smallest → round → formula
  • Molecular formula = empirical formula × n (n from molar mass)
