KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Matter = anything with mass that occupies space
  • 3 states: Solid (fixed shape + volume), Liquid (fixed volume), Gas (neither)
  • Matter → Pure Substances OR Mixtures
  • Pure Substances → Elements (one atom type) OR Compounds (two+ elements, chemically bonded)
  • Mixtures → Homogeneous (uniform) OR Heterogeneous (non-uniform)
  • Compounds have DIFFERENT properties from their constituent elements
