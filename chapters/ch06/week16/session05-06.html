6.2-6.3 Chemical Equilibrium, Law of Mass Action

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Chemical equilibrium: forward and reverse reaction rates become equal, both reactants and products present
  • Law of Mass Action: K = [products]^coeff / [reactants]^coeff
  • K is constant at a fixed temperature, regardless of starting concentrations
  • Pure solids and liquids are excluded from the equilibrium constant expression
