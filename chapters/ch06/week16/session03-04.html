6.1 Equilibrium in Physical Processes

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Dynamic equilibrium: forward and backward processes continue at exactly equal rates
  • Macroscopic properties (amounts, pressure) stay constant at equilibrium, even as molecular change continues
  • Equilibrium requires a CLOSED system — open systems never reach a stable balance
  • Vapour pressure: constant at a given temperature for liquid-vapour equilibrium
