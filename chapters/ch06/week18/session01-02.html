KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Inert gas at constant volume: no effect on equilibrium (concentrations of reacting species unchanged)
  • Catalyst: speeds up both forward and reverse reactions equally, no effect on K or equilibrium position
  • Temperature: the only factor that changes the actual VALUE of K
  • Concentration/pressure changes shift equilibrium position but never change K at constant temperature
