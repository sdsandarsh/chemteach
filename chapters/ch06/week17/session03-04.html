6.6 Applications of Equilibrium Constants

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Reaction Quotient Q: same formula as K, but uses current (non-equilibrium) concentrations
  • Q < K: reaction proceeds forward; Q > K: reaction proceeds backward; Q = K: at equilibrium
  • Large K favours products strongly; small K favours reactants strongly
  • K applications: predicting direction, calculating equilibrium concentrations, extent of reaction
