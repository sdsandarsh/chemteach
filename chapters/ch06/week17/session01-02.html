6.4-6.5 Homogeneous, Heterogeneous Equilibria

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Homogeneous equilibrium: all species in the same phase
  • Heterogeneous equilibrium: species span multiple phases (e.g. solid + gas)
  • Pure solids/liquids are excluded from K — their concentration never changes
  • Only gases and dissolved species appear explicitly in heterogeneous K expressions
