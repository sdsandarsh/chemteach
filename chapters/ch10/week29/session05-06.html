Topics covered today:
9.3 Alkenes: Nomenclature, Preparation

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Alkenes (CnH2n): contain one C=C double bond, planar sp2 geometry
  • Prepared via dehydrohalogenation (haloalkane + alcoholic KOH) or dehydration (alcohol + H2SO4, heat)
  • Both methods follow Saytzeff's Rule: more substituted alkene is the major product
  • C=C double bond enables addition reactions, unlike saturated alkanes
