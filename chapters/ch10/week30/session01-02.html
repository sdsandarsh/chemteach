KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Markovnikov's Rule: H adds to the carbon with more hydrogens; X adds to the carbon with fewer
  • True mechanism: reaction proceeds via the more stable carbocation intermediate
  • Anti-Markovnikov addition occurs with HBr + peroxides (free radical mechanism)
  • Symmetrical alkenes give only one product, regardless of Markovnikov considerations
