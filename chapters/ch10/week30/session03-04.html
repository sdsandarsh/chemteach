Topics covered today:
9.3 Ozonolysis, Polymerisation

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Ozonolysis: O3 then Zn/H2O cleaves C=C completely into two carbonyl fragments
  • Used analytically to determine the exact position of a double bond
  • Polymerisation: many alkene monomers link via addition to form one long polymer chain
  • Polyethylene is manufactured industrially via addition polymerisation of ethene
