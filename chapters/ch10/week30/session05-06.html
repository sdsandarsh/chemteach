9.4 Alkynes: Nomenclature, Acetylene Preparation

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Alkynes (CnH2n-2): contain a C≡C triple bond, sp hybridized, linear 180° geometry
  • Acetylene (ethyne) prepared from calcium carbide + water
  • Triple bond = 1 sigma + 2 pi bonds, more reactive than alkenes
  • Naming follows the same IUPAC rules, with '-yne' suffix indicating the triple bond
