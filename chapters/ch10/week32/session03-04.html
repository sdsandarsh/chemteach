Topics covered today:
9.5.6 Directive Influence of Substituents

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Electron-donating groups (-OH, -NH2, -CH3) activate the ring, direct ortho/para
  • Electron-withdrawing groups (-NO2, -COOH) deactivate the ring, direct meta
  • Halogens are an exception: deactivating overall but still ortho/para-directing
  • Directive influence arises from uneven resonance electron distribution, not uniform ring activation
