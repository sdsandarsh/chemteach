9.5.5 Electrophilic Substitution Mechanism

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Electrophilic Aromatic Substitution (EAS): electrophile attacks, then proton is lost
  • Arenium ion intermediate temporarily disrupts aromaticity, stabilized by resonance
  • Net result: substitution occurs, full aromaticity is restored in the product
  • This mechanism explains why benzene favours substitution over addition
