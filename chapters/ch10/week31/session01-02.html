Topics covered today:
9.4.4 Alkyne Reactions

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Alkynes can undergo TWO sequential addition reactions (triple → double → single bond)
  • Both additions follow Markovnikov's Rule, same underlying mechanism as alkenes
  • Amount of reagent (limited vs excess) controls whether reaction stops at one or two additions
  • Alkynes are generally more reactive than alkenes in addition reactions
