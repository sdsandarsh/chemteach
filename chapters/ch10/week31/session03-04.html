KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Kekule's structure: hexagonal ring with alternating single/double bonds
  • Modern structure: delocalized pi electrons spread evenly around the ring (resonance hybrid)
  • All 6 C-C bonds in benzene are identical in length, proving delocalization
  • Delocalization gives benzene extra stability (resonance energy), favouring substitution over addition
