KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Huckel's Rule: aromaticity requires planar, fully conjugated ring with exactly (4n+2) pi electrons
  • Benzene satisfies all three conditions (6 pi electrons, n=1) — fully aromatic
  • Cyclooctatetraene (8 pi electrons) fails the 4n+2 condition — not aromatic
  • Benzene prepared industrially from coal tar, or via cyclic trimerization of acetylene
