KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Classical redox: oxidation = gain of oxygen; reduction = loss of oxygen
  • Electronic redox: oxidation = loss of electrons; reduction = gain of electrons (universal definition)
  • Oxidation number: bookkeeping charge tracking electron transfer in any compound
  • Oxidizing agent gets reduced; reducing agent gets oxidized (OIL RIG)
