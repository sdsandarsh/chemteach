KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Qualitative analysis identifies WHICH elements are present in an organic compound
  • Lassaigne's test: fuse with sodium to convert N, S, halogens into testable ions
  • Nitrogen → NaCN (Prussian blue test); Sulfur → Na2S (lead acetate test); Halogen → NaX (AgNO3 test)
  • Covalent organic elements must be converted to ionic form before standard tests can detect them
