Topics covered today:
9.2 Alkanes: Nomenclature, Isomerism, Conformations

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Alkanes: CnH2n+2, saturated acyclic hydrocarbons with only C-C and C-H single bonds
  • Chain isomerism in alkanes follows the same principles learned in Chapter 9
  • Conformations: different 3D shapes from free rotation around C-C sigma bonds (staggered, eclipsed)
  • Staggered conformation is more stable than eclipsed due to less electron repulsion
  • This bridges Chapter 9's principles directly into Chapter 10: Hydrocarbons
