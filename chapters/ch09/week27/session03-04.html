KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Substitution: one atom/group replaces another (alkanes, aromatic compounds)
  • Addition: atoms add across a double/triple bond (alkenes, alkynes)
  • Elimination: atoms removed, forming a new double bond (reverse of addition)
  • Aromatic rings favour substitution over addition to preserve their stable pi system
