KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Inductive effect: permanent electron pull through sigma bonds, weakens with distance
  • Resonance effect: electron pair delocalization through a conjugated pi system
  • Hyperconjugation: adjacent C-H sigma bonds stabilize an empty/partial orbital
  • More alkyl groups (more hyperconjugation) means more stable carbocations: tertiary > secondary > primary
