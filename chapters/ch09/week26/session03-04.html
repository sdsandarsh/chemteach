8.6 Isomerism

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Isomers: same molecular formula, different structure/arrangement
  • Structural isomerism: chain, position, and functional isomerism (different connectivity)
  • Stereoisomerism: same connectivity, different 3D spatial arrangement (geometric, optical)
  • Optical isomers are non-superimposable mirror images (like hands)
