KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • d-orbital hybridisation extends beyond sp3 when more than 4 bonds/lone pairs are needed
  • sp3d: 5 orbitals, trigonal bipyramidal geometry (e.g. PCl5)
  • sp3d2: 6 orbitals, octahedral geometry (e.g. SF6)
  • Only elements with accessible d-orbitals (period 3+) can form expanded octets
