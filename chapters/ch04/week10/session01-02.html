KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Lewis structures show valence electrons as dots, bonding pairs as shared dots/lines
  • Method: count valence e- → skeleton → distribute to satisfy octets → verify
  • Formal charge = V − L − B/2, helps choose the most accurate structure
  • Most stable structure: formal charges near zero, negative charge on most electronegative atom
