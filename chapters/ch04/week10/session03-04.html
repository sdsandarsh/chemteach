KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Ionic bond: complete transfer of electrons forming oppositely charged ions held by electrostatic attraction
  • Lattice energy: energy released forming a crystal lattice from gaseous ions; increases with charge, decreases with ionic size
  • Fajans' Rules: small, highly charged cation + large anion → more covalent character in an 'ionic' bond
  • Ionic bonding typically occurs between metals (low ionization energy) and non-metals (high electron affinity)
