4.3 Bond Parameters, Resonance, Dipole Moment

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Bond parameters: bond length, bond angle, bond enthalpy, bond order
  • Resonance: true structure is a stable hybrid of multiple valid Lewis structures (e.g. ozone)
  • Dipole moment: measures bond/molecular polarity; depends on charge separation AND geometry
  • Symmetric molecules (CO2, CCl4) can have zero net dipole moment despite polar bonds
