4.4 VSEPR Theory

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • VSEPR: electron pairs around a central atom repel and arrange for maximum separation
  • Repulsion strength: lone-lone > lone-bond > bond-bond
  • Lone pairs compress bond angles below the 'ideal' geometric value
  • Shape predicted by total electron pairs: 2=linear, 3=trigonal planar, 4=tetrahedral
