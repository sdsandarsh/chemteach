KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Hybridisation: mixing of atomic orbitals to form new, identical hybrid orbitals before bonding
  • sp: 2 orbitals, 180°, linear (e.g. BeCl2)
  • sp2: 3 orbitals, 120°, trigonal planar (e.g. BF3)
  • sp3: 4 orbitals, 109.5°, tetrahedral (e.g. CH4)
