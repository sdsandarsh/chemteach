4.5 Valence Bond Theory, Sigma and Pi Bonds

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Valence Bond Theory: bonds form from overlap of atomic orbitals
  • Sigma bond: head-on overlap, strong, allows free rotation
  • Pi bond: sideways overlap of parallel p-orbitals, weaker, prevents free rotation
  • Single = 1σ, Double = 1σ+1π, Triple = 1σ+2π
