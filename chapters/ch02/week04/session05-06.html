2.2 Atomic Models: Thomson, Rutherford

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Thomson's model: positive charge spread uniformly, electrons embedded within it (plum pudding)
  • Gold foil experiment disproved Thomson's model
  • Rutherford's model: tiny dense positive nucleus, electrons orbit at a distance, mostly empty space
  • This nuclear model became the foundation for all later atomic models
