KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Cathode ray tube experiments revealed the electron (negative, near-zero mass)
  • Anode/canal ray experiments revealed the proton (positive, ~1u mass)
  • Chadwick later discovered the neutron (neutral, ~1u mass)
  • Atoms are divisible — built from these three sub-atomic particles
