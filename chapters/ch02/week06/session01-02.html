KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Continuous spectrum: all wavelengths blend smoothly (e.g. sunlight through a prism)
  • Line spectrum: only specific, discrete wavelengths appear — unique to each element
  • Hydrogen's line spectrum revealed quantized energy levels within the atom
  • Spectral series (Lyman, Balmer, Paschen) correspond to transitions ending at specific energy levels
