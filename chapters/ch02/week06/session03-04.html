KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Bohr's model: electrons orbit only in specific, quantized energy levels (n=1,2,3...)
  • Electrons in allowed orbits do not radiate energy — they are stable
  • Transitions between orbits emit/absorb photons of exact energy, explaining the line spectrum
  • Energy of orbit n in hydrogen: En = -13.6/n² eV
