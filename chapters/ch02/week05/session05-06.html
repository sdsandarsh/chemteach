2.3.2 Photoelectric Effect, Dual Behaviour of Radiation

✓ SESSION COMPLETE

KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Photoelectric effect: light ejects electrons from a metal surface only above a threshold frequency
  • Einstein explained this using photons: E = hν, discrete energy packets
  • Brightness (intensity) increases photon count, not photon energy — frequency alone determines ejection
  • This proved light has dual nature: wave AND particle behaviour
