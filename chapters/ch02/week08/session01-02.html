KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • 4 quantum numbers (n, l, m, s) uniquely describe every electron in an atom
  • n = shell, l = shape (s,p,d,f), m = orientation, s = spin
  • Pauli Exclusion Principle: no two electrons share all 4 quantum numbers
  • This completes Chapter 2 (Atomic Structure) and opens the door to the Periodic Table
