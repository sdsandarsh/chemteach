KEY CONCEPTS FROM TODAY:
  • Quantum mechanics replaces orbits with orbitals (probability clouds, |\u03c8|\u00b2).
  • 4 Quantum numbers (n, l, m_l, m_s) uniquely describe every electron.
  • Node formulas: Radial = n-l-1, Angular = l. Total = n-1.
  • Aufbau: Lowest energy first. Pauli: Max 2 electrons/orbital, opposite spin. Hund: Singly fill degenerate orbitals first.
  • Half-filled (d\u2075) and fully-filled (d\u00b9\u2070) subshells offer extra stability due to symmetry and exchange energy.
